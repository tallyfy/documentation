## Pricing for Manufactory Observability Platform

Manufactory provides a customized pricing structure based on your specific implementation and usage requirements. Our pricing factors include:

### Pricing Factors

- **Data volume** - The number of events collected and processed
- **Storage duration** - How long your data is retained
- **API call frequency** - The rate of data transmission to our platform
- **Integration options** - Which collection methods you utilize (REST API, Websocket, CLI agent)

### How to Get Pricing Information

To receive a personalized quote for your Manufactory implementation:

1. **Contact our sales team** - Reach out through our [contact page](https://tallyfy.com/contact-us/)
2. **Schedule a consultation** - Our team will discuss your specific requirements
3. **Receive a customized quote** - Based on your expected usage pattern and needs

For immediate assistance with pricing questions, please email [sales@tallyfy.com](mailto:sales@tallyfy.com).
