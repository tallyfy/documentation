## How to change the work week

Worried about task deadlines falling on non-working days? No worries! With Tallyfy's 'Set Work Week' feature, you can automatically adjust deadlines. Here's how:

1. Go to 'Settings' in the top right corner of your screen.

2. Click on the 'Organization' tab and then 'Customization'.

3. Look for 'Default work week' and choose the option that suits your needs.

4. Simply select the option and Tallyfy will automatically update the deadlines for you. No more confusion!
