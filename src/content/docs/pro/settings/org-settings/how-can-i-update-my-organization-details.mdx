## Updating organization details

Only Tallyfy administrators can change your organization's name, address, city, state, country, zip code and other details. Here's how.

<Steps>
1. Click **Settings** (usually at the bottom left).
2. Under the **My Organization** heading, click **Org Details**.

   ![Org details settings page](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-change-org-details.png)

3. Change the organization's name or other details as needed. There's a 50-character limit on the name field.
4. Click **Save**.
</Steps>

:::note[Organization name limitations]
**Can't change your organization name?** During initial setup, the organization name field may be locked in the UI. Contact [Tallyfy support](/products/pro/miscellaneous/support/how-can-i-contact-tallyfys-support-team/) if you need help changing it.
:::
