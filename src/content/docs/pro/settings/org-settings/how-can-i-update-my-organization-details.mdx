## How to find and update your organization details

Administrators can update basic details about their organization in Tallyfy.

<Steps>
1. Click **Settings** (usually at the bottom left).
2. Under the **My Organization** heading, click **Org Details**.

   ![Organization Details settings page](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-change-org-details.png)

3. Change the organization's name or other details shown as needed.
4. Click **Save**.
</Steps>
