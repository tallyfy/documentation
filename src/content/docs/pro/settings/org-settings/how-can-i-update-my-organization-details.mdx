## How to find and update organization details in Settings

To update your organization details, follow these steps:

<Steps>
1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Click on **My Organization > Org Details** and make the necessary changes.

   ![Organization Details](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-change-org-details.png)
3. Don't forget to **Save** your changes.
</Steps>
