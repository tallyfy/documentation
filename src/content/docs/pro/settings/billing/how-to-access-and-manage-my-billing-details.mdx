## How to access and manage billing details

Welcome to Tallyfy - the cloud-based product that simplifies your work! In this article, we'll guide you through accessing and managing your billing details.

To get started, follow these simple steps:

1. Click on **Settings** at the bottom of the left sidebar.
2. Once you're in Settings, click on **Billing**. Here, you'll find all the information related to your billing.

![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-click-billing-settings.png)

3. In the Billing section, you'll be able to edit your payment card, view all billing history, change your current plan and billing details, and even access a link to allow someone else to pay for you!

![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-show-billing-info.png)

That's it! You now have easy access to all your billing details, making it simpler than ever to keep track of your plan, invoices, and payments.

If you're not finding what you need or have any questions, issues, or feature requests, don't hesitate to reach out to Tallyfy Support. Our friendly team is always here to assist you. Simply report a help ticket from within the app, and we'll be happy to help!
