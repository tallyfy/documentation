## How to redeem a coupon

If you have a billing coupon - just enter it at checkout when you purchase a plan. If you have any issues - please raise a ticket with support (see below to contact us).

## Fair Price Guarantee

Tallyfy offers a unique program open to certain countries in the world - where the company signing up has a _primary country_ set to that country. Eligibility for certain countries gives you _90% off for life_. More information is [here](https://tallyfy.com/pricing/fair-price-guarantee/).
