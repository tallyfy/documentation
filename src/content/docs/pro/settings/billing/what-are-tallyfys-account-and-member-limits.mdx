## What are Tallyfy's account and member limits

### Member Roles and Pricing

Tallyfy offers two types of member roles with specific pricing:

- **Full Members**: $300 per annum
- **Light Members**: $50 per annum

A Data Feed option is available for $20 per member per annum, which feeds Tallyfy data to your BI or analytics platform.

### Role Capabilities

| Features           | Light Member | Full Member |
| ------------------ | ------------ | ----------- |
| Create Templates   | No           | Yes         |
| Edit Templates     | No           | Yes         |
| Launch and Assign  | Yes          | Yes         |
| Track and Complete | Yes          | Yes         |
| Reminders          | Yes          | Yes         |
| Watching           | Yes          | Yes         |

### Trial Plan Limits

If you're on a Tallyfy trial, you have the following limits:

- 10 members
- 10 guests
- 100 total task assignments of all kinds
- 10 active processes

If you find these trial limits restrictive, you can contact Tallyfy support with a note explaining your needs. The support team may consider lifting certain trial limits based on your specific requirements.

### Members vs. Guests

- **Members**: Employees or people within your company who have access to Tallyfy. Billing is based on member type (Full or Light).
- **Guests**: External users with free and unlimited access. They can be clients invited to fill out a form or perform a specific task in a process.

For more detailed pricing information, visit our [pricing page](https://tallyfy.com/pricing/).
