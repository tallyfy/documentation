## How to update your login email address

Your email address is your Tallyfy login. This guide explains how to change it.

### Prerequisites

*   You must log in to your Tallyfy account using your current email.
*   You need access to *both* your old and new email inboxes.

### Steps to update your email address

<Steps>
1. Click your profile picture or name (top right).
2. Choose **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
3. In the **Settings** menu (on the left), choose **My Profile**.
4. Make sure you are on the **My Details** tab.
5. Find the **Email** field and type in the new email address.
6. Scroll down and click **Save Changes**.
7. You should see a confirmation message.
</Steps>

### Verify and use your new email

<Steps>
1. Log out of Tallyfy.
2. Log back in using your **new** email address and your current Tallyfy password.
3. Check your **new** email inbox for any verification emails from Tallyfy, although this isn't always required.
</Steps>

:::note[Limits on email changes]
For security reasons, you can only change your email address 3 times per day. Contact support if you need to change it more than this.
:::

### If you have problems

*   Double-check that you typed the new email address correctly.
*   Confirm you can receive emails at the new address (check spam folders).
*   If you cannot log in after changing the email, [contact Tallyfy Support](/products/pro/miscellaneous/support/how-can-i-contact-tallyfys-support-team/) and provide both your old and new email addresses.
