## How can you change email address

To change your email address in Tallyfy, follow these simple steps:

1. Go to **Settings** and select **My Profile** followed by **My Details**
2. In the email address text box, enter your new email address
3. Don't forget to click on **SAVE CHANGES**
4. Now, log out and log back in using your new email address
