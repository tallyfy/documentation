## How to change your email address in Tallyfy

Your email address serves as your primary login identifier in Tallyfy. Follow these steps to update it:

### Prerequisites

- You must be logged into your Tallyfy account
- You need access to both your current and new email addresses

### Update your email address

1. Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner of the screen
2. Select **Settings** from the dropdown menu
3. Choose **My Profile** from the left sidebar
4. Select the **My Details** tab
5. In the **Email** field, replace your current email address with your new one
6. Click **Save Changes** at the bottom of the form
7. You'll see a confirmation message that your changes have been saved

### Verify and use your new email

1. Log out of your Tallyfy account
2. Log back in using your new email address and your existing password
3. Check your new email inbox for any verification messages from Tallyfy

:::note[Email change limits]
For security reasons, Tallyfy limits email address changes to 3 times per day. If you need to make additional changes, please [contact support](/products/pro/miscellaneous/support/how-can-i-contact-tallyfys-support-team/).
:::

### Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues:
- Ensure you have entered the correct email address without typos
- Check that you can access the new email account
- If you can't log in with your new email, contact [Tallyfy Support](/products/pro/miscellaneous/support/how-can-i-contact-tallyfys-support-team/) ([support@tallyfy.com](mailto:support@tallyfy.com)) and provide both your old and new email addresses
