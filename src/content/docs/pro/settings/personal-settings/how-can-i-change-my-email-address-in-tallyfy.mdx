## How can I update my login email address in Tallyfy?

Your email address serves as your Tallyfy login credential. This comprehensive guide explains how to safely change your email address.

### What are the prerequisites for changing my email address?

*   You must be logged in to your Tallyfy account using your current email address
*   You need access to *both* your current and new email inboxes for verification purposes

### What are the steps to update my email address?

<Steps>
1. Click your profile picture or name located in the top right corner.
2. Choose **Settings** from the dropdown menu that appears.
3. In the **Settings** menu (on the left side), select **My Profile**.
4. Ensure you are on the **My Details** tab.
5. Locate the **Email** field and enter your new email address.
6. Scroll down and click **Save Changes** to confirm.
7. You should see a confirmation message indicating the change was successful.
</Steps>

### How do I verify and use my new email address?

<Steps>
1. Log out of Tallyfy completely.
2. Log back in using your **new** email address and your current Tallyfy password.
3. Check your **new** email inbox for any verification emails from Tallyfy, although this verification step isn't always required.
</Steps>

:::note[Limits on email changes]
For security reasons, you can only change your email address 3 times per day. Contact support if you need to exceed this limit.
:::

### What should I do if I encounter problems?

*   Double-check that you typed the new email address correctly without any typos
*   Confirm you can receive emails at the new address (check spam folders thoroughly)
*   If you cannot log in after changing the email, [contact Tallyfy Support](/products/pro/miscellaneous/support/how-can-i-contact-tallyfys-support-team/) and provide both your old and new email addresses for assistance
