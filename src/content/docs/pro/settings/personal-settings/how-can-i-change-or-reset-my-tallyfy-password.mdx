## How do I update my Tallyfy password?

Tallyfy offers two ways to update your password: changing it while logged in or resetting it if you can't access your account.

### Change your password while logged in

Follow these steps to update your password when you're already logged into Tallyfy:

<Steps>
1. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner of the screen
2. Select **Settings** from the dropdown menu
3. Choose **My Profile** from the left sidebar
4. Click the **Security** tab
5. Enter your current password in the **Current Password** field
6. Enter your new password in the **New Password** field
7. Re-enter the same password in the **Confirm Password** field
8. Click **Save Changes**
</Steps>

### Reset a forgotten password

If you can't log in because you've forgotten your password:

<Steps>
1. Visit the Tallyfy sign-in page: [https://go.tallyfy.com/#/sign-in](https://go.tallyfy.com/#/sign-in)
2. Click **Forgot password?** below the sign-in form
3. Enter the email address associated with your Tallyfy account
4. Click **Send Reset Link**
5. Check your email inbox for a message from Tallyfy
   - Also check your spam/junk folders if you don't see it
   - The link in this email is valid for 24 hours
6. Click the password reset link in the email
7. On the reset page, enter your new password twice
8. Click **Reset Password**
9. You'll be redirected to the login page to sign in with your new password
</Steps>

### Password requirements

For [security](/products/pro/compliance/), Tallyfy requires that passwords:
- Be at least 8 characters long
- Include at least one uppercase letter
- Include at least one lowercase letter
- Include at least one number
- Not be the same as your previous password

:::note[Password change limits]
For security reasons, password changes are limited to 3 times per day. If you need additional changes, please [contact Tallyfy support](/products/pro/miscellaneous/support/how-can-i-contact-tallyfys-support-team/).
:::

### Troubleshooting

If you experience issues resetting your password:
- Ensure you're using the correct email address for your account
- Check that the reset link hasn't expired (links are valid for 24 hours)
- If you don't receive a reset email, contact [Tallyfy Support](/products/pro/miscellaneous/support/how-can-i-contact-tallyfys-support-team/) with:
  - Your name
  - Your organization name
  - All possible email addresses you might have used
