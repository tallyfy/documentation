## How to Change or Reset Your Password in Tallyfy

Changing your password in Tallyfy is easy! Whether you want to update your current password or reset it because you're locked out, we've got you covered. Just follow these simple steps:

**Changing Your Password:**

1. Log in to your Tallyfy account and go to the 'My Profile' page in Settings.
2. Click on the option to set a new password.
3. Follow the instructions provided to create and save your new password.
4. Remember to keep your new password in a safe place.

**Resetting Your Password:**

If you've locked yourself out by entering the wrong password, don't worry! Just follow these steps:

1. Go to the Tallyfy sign-in page: [https://go.tallyfy.com/#/sign-in](https://go.tallyfy.com/#/sign-in).
2. Click on 'Forgot password?'.
3. Enter the email address associated with your account.
4. Check your email for a password reset link from Tallyfy (remember to also check your junk/spam mail folder).
5. Click on the link in the email to open the password reset page.
6. Follow the instructions to create a new password.
7. Log in to Tallyfy using your new password.
8. Make sure to note down your new password in a safe place.

If you still encounter any issues with logging in, don't hesitate to contact our support team at support@tallyfy.com. Please provide a list of possible email addresses and the name of your organization or other users to assist us in investigating the issue.

If you have any other questions, issues, or feature requests, feel free to report a help ticket to Tallyfy Support. We're here to assist you!
