## Manage your individual account settings

This section covers settings that apply only to your own Tallyfy<sup>®</sup> user account. You can customize things like your profile details, password, notifications, and preferred language here.

Explore the articles below to learn about managing your personal Tallyfy<sup>®</sup> experience.
