## How can I manage my personal Tallyfy settings?

This section covers individual Tallyfy account settings that only affect your personal user experience and preferences.

Personal settings let you customize profile details, password management, notification preferences, language selection, and other individual account configurations. These options help you optimize your personal Tallyfy workflow experience to match your work style.
