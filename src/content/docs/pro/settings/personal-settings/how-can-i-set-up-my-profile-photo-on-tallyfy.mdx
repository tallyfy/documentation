## How to update your profile picture

Welcome to Tallyfy! Let's make your profile pop with a custom profile photo. It's an easy way to personalize your Tallyfy experience and make it easier to identify who's assigned to what tasks. Plus, it adds a nice touch to your emails too!

Here's how to set up your profile photo:

1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Open **My Account > Profile** and click on **CHANGE PHOTO**.

   ![Change Photo](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-change-profile-picture1701983252467.png)

3. Upload a photo and click **SAVE CHANGES**. Now, your photo will appear on your assigned tasks and in your emails.

So go ahead and upload your most fabulous photo! If you have any questions, issues, or feature requests, don't hesitate to reach out to Tallyfy Support. We're here to help and would love to hear from you.
