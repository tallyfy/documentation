## How to upload or change your profile picture

Adding a profile picture helps your teammates recognize you in Tallyfy. Your picture appears next to tasks you're assigned to and in some system emails.

Here's how to add or change your photo:

<Steps>
1. Click **Settings** (usually at the bottom left).
2. Go to **My Account** > **Profile**.
3. Click the **CHANGE PHOTO** button.
   ![Change Photo button on Profile page](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-change-profile-picture1701983252467.png)
4. Choose an image file from your computer to upload.
5. Click **SAVE CHANGES**.
</Steps>

Your new photo will now appear in Tallyfy.
