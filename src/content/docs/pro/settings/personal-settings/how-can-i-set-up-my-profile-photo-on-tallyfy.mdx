## Uploading or changing your profile picture

Adding a profile picture helps your teammates recognize you in Tallyfy. Your picture appears next to tasks you're assigned to and in some system emails. This visual identification improves team collaboration and communication.

Here's how to add or change your photo:

<Steps>
1. Click **Settings** (usually at the bottom left).
2. Go to **My Account** > **Profile**.
3. Click the **CHANGE PHOTO** button.
   ![](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-change-profile-picture1701983252467.png)
4. Choose an image file from your computer to upload.
5. Click **SAVE CHANGES**.
</Steps>

Your new photo will now appear throughout Tallyfy, helping teammates easily identify your contributions and assignments.
