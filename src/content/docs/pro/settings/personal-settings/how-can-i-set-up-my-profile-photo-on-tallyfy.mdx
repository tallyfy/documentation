## How to update your profile picture

Personalizing your Tallyfy profile with a photo helps team members identify you in tasks and communications. Your profile photo appears on assigned tasks and in system-generated emails.

Here's how to set up your profile photo:

1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Open **My Account > Profile** and click on **CHANGE PHOTO**.

   ![Change Photo](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-change-profile-picture1701983252467.png)

3. Upload a photo and click **SAVE CHANGES**. Now, your photo will appear on your assigned tasks and in your emails.
