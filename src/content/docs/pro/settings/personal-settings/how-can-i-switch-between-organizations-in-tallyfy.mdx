## How to switch between organizations

Tallyfy makes it easy for you to manage different companies and organizations. With our new Organization Switcher feature, you can now easily switch between different organizations within Tallyfy.

To access the Organization Switcher, click on your profile picture on the sidebar, and then, select **Switch Organizations**. There, you will find a list of all the organizations that you are a member or a guest of. Simply click on the organization name that you want to work with, and you're all set!

![Organization Switcher](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-switch-organizations.png)

This makes it convenient for those who work for or manage multiple companies to easily navigate between different company accounts in Tallyfy.

So go ahead and explore the different organizations and effortlessly manage your tasks, processes, and templates for each company using Tallyfy!
