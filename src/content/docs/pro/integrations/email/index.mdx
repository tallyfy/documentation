## Email Integration Options

Tallyfy provides multiple ways to integrate with email systems, enhancing communication between team members and external stakeholders throughout your workflows.

### Custom SMTP Configuration

Configure Tallyfy to use your organization's email servers instead of the default Tallyfy mail service. Benefits include:

- **Consistent branding** - Emails appear from your company domain
- **Enhanced deliverability** - Leverage your existing email reputation
- **Security compliance** - Keep communications within your infrastructure
- **Email filtering control** - Apply your organizational email policies

### Gmail Add-on Integration

The Tallyfy Gmail add-on enables direct workflow interaction from within your Gmail inbox:

- Launch processes directly from emails
- Create tasks based on email content
- Track workflow progress without leaving Gmail
- Streamline communication workflows

### Email Notification Management

Tallyfy provides granular control over which workflow events trigger email notifications, allowing each user to customize their notification preferences based on their specific needs.
