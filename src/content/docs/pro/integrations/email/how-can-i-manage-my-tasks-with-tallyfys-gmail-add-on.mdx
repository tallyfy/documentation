## Using the Tallyfy Gmail Add-on

The Tallyfy Gmail Add-on lets you manage Tallyfy tasks and processes directly from your Gmail inbox, saving you from switching back and forth between apps.

### What you need

- A Google Workspace (formerly G Suite) or a standard Gmail account.
- An active Tallyfy account.
- Permission from your Google Workspace admin to install add-ons (if applicable).
- Basic knowledge of how Tallyfy works.

### Key features

- **Create tasks**: Turn an email into a Tallyfy task easily.
- **Launch processes**: Start a Tallyfy process based on an email.
- **Track status**: Check on your Tallyfy tasks from within Gmail.
- **Save time**: Less switching between Tallyfy and Gmail.
- **Keep context**: The original email stays linked to the task or process you create.

### How to install it

<Steps>
1. Go to the [Tallyfy Add-on page in the Google Workspace Marketplace](https://gsuite.google.com/marketplace/app/tallyfy/671710262095/).
2. Click **Install**.
3. Follow the steps to give the add-on permission to access your Gmail and Tallyfy info.
4. After installation, you should see the Tallyfy icon in the right sidebar of your Gmail.
</Steps>

:::note[Admins Installing for Everyone]
Google Workspace admins can install the add-on for everyone in the organization via the Admin Console (Apps > Marketplace Apps).
:::

### Using the add-on in Gmail

When you open an email in Gmail, you'll see the Tallyfy icon in the sidebar on the right.

<Steps>
1. Click the Tallyfy icon to open the Tallyfy panel.
2. If it asks you to, sign in to your Tallyfy account.
3. Choose what you want to do: **Create Task**, **Launch Process**, or **View Tasks**.
</Steps>

![Tallyfy Gmail Add-on panel](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/file-orF3UnYz5p.png)

### Creating a Tallyfy Task from an email

<Steps>
1. Click **Create Task** in the Tallyfy panel.
2. Give the task a name (it will suggest the email subject).
3. Set a due date if you need one.
4. Add any extra notes.
5. Click **Create**.
6. Tallyfy creates the task and attaches the email content for reference.
</Steps>

### Starting a Tallyfy Process from an email

<Steps>
1. Click **Launch Process** in the Tallyfy panel.
2. Choose the Tallyfy template you want to use.
3. Fill out any required starting information.
4. Choose who to assign the process or tasks to.
5. Click **Launch**.
</Steps>

### Checking your Tallyfy tasks

<Steps>
1. Click **View Tasks** in the Tallyfy panel.
2. See a list of your assigned Tallyfy tasks.
3. Check their status and due dates.
4. Click a task to see more details.
</Steps>

### Troubleshooting tips

| Problem                 | Possible Solution                                    |
|-------------------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| Tallyfy icon missing    | Refresh Gmail, clear browser cache/cookies.          |
| Can't sign in / errors | Try signing out and back into the add-on.            |
| Can't create tasks      | Check your permissions within your Tallyfy account.  |
| Add-on is slow        | Check your internet connection.                      |

### Where it works

The Tallyfy Gmail Add-on works best in:
- The Gmail website on your computer.
- It also works in the mobile Gmail app, but might have fewer features there.
