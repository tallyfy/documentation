## Automate monthly process launches with Zapier

Tallyfy can automatically [launch processes](/products/pro/launching/) every month through Zapier's schedule trigger. It takes about 5 minutes to set up, and you won't have to remember to kick off monthly reporting, reviews, or any other recurring workflow again.

:::note[Why consider alternative integration platforms?]
Zapier handles basic connections well, but for business-critical integrations we'd recommend [middleware](/products/pro/integrations/middleware/) platforms like **n8n**, **Make**, or **Microsoft Power Automate** - they're more capable and cost-effective.

If you've got developers, Tallyfy's [Open API](/products/pro/integrations/open-api/) lets you build custom integrations directly.
:::

<Steps>
1. Pick the **Schedule** trigger in Zapier.

    ![Zapier schedule trigger selection](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy/pro/zapier-schedule-trigger-select.gif)
2. Set the day of the month and time of day you want.

    ![Zapier schedule day and time configuration](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy/pro/zapier-schedule-day-time-select.gif)
3. Choose the Tallyfy **Launch Process** action.

    ![Tallyfy Launch Process action in Zapier](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy/pro/zapier-launch-process-action.gif)
4. Select the [template](/products/pro/documenting/templates/) and give the [process](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/processes/) a name.

    ![Choosing template name for scheduled process](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy/pro/zapier-choose-template-name.gif)
5. Test the Zap, name it, and turn it on.

    ![Testing and enabling the Zap](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy/pro/zapier-test-and-enable.gif)
</Steps>
