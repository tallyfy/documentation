## Automation examples

This section provides practical examples of how to implement automation rules in Tallyfy to solve common business challenges.

### Prerequisites
- Basic understanding of Tallyfy automation concepts
- Access to create or edit templates
- Familiarity with form fields and steps

### Benefits of studying automation examples
- **Faster implementation** - Learn from proven automation patterns
- **Creative solutions** - Discover new ways to automate your processes
- **Problem-solving** - Find examples that match your specific needs
- **Learning aid** - Understand complex automation concepts through practical scenarios

### Example categories
The automation examples in this section cover various business scenarios:
- Conditional approval flows
- Dynamic task assignment
- Form-based routing
- Department-specific process variations
- Status-triggered notifications
- Time-based escalations

For advanced process mapping concepts and BPMN patterns, refer to our [BPMN Examples and Patterns](https://tallyfy.com/bpmn-examples-and-patterns) article.
