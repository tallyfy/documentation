## How can Powertools help me

In this article, we'll talk about the amazing Power Tools in Tallyfy that can make your life easier when it comes to building templates, creating assignments, and setting deadlines. With Power Tools, you can streamline your workflow and save time.

To access Power Tools, simply go to "Config" in the Template Editor.

Let's take a look at what you can do with Power Tools:

**1. Build steps quickly**: Easily create steps for your workflow by copy-pasting or typing in step titles. Once you've entered all the steps, click on "BUILD".

![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-click-powertools-create-steps.png)

**2. Create assignments instantly**: Assign steps to team members effortlessly. Simply select the member name from the dropdown menu and click "Assign". Once all assignments are created, click "APPLY".

![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-click-powertools-assign.png)

**3. Changing deadlines in bulk**: Easily adjust deadlines for your tasks. You can add or subtract minutes, hours, days, weeks, or months. Once all deadlines are set, click "UPDATE".

![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-click-powertools-deadlines.png)

**4. Pushing template into co-worker's favorite**: Sometimes, you may want to highlight a specific template for one or more co-workers by adding it to their favorites tab. You can use the bulk favorite power tool to do that. Simply add their names to the text box, and the template will be added to their favorites!
![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-click-powertools-favorite.png)


With Power Tools, you can make editing assignments and deadlines quick and effortless.

Give it a try and see how Tallyfy's Power Tools can supercharge your productivity!
