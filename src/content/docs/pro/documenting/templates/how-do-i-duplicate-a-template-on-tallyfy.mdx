## How to duplicate templates on Tallyfy

Duplicating templates allows you to create variations of existing processes without building them from scratch. This feature is useful when you need to make modifications to a standard process for specific use cases.

## Duplication steps

To duplicate a template:

1. Navigate to the Library section where your templates are stored
2. Select the template you want to duplicate
3. Click **More > Duplicate**
4. Enter a name for the new template
5. Click **Duplicate** to create the copy

![Duplicate template](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-duplicate-template.png)

## Important considerations

When duplicating templates, be aware of these limitations:

* Tags are not copied to the new template
* Step webhooks and template webhooks are not transferred
* Form field aliases and step aliases are assigned new unique identifiers in the duplicated template

These unique identifiers help prevent integration conflicts when setting up connections to external systems.
