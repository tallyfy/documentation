## How to make a template available for the general public

With Tallyfy, you can easily share your templates with anyone - even if they're not on Tallyfy! Whether it's for clients or the general public, you can make your template world-visible in just a few steps.

1. Open your Library view and select the template you want to share.
2. Click on **Edit** and then choose **Config**.
3. Select the permissions section.
4. Select **Public to the world** and mark it as **yes**
   ![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-public-template.png)

Feel proud of contributing to the world and your peer group! Make sure to share the link with your LinkedIn connections, social media followers, and co-workers. Thanks for being awesome!
