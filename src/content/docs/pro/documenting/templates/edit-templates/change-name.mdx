## How to rename your template

:::note[Changes made won't affect ongoing processes]

Note that any change you make to a [template](/products/pro/documenting/templates/) won't affect any ongoing [process](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/processes/) that depends on it. This ensures the integrity and consistency of processes.

:::

To rename a [template](/products/pro/documenting/templates/), head over to the **Config** button, and then click on your template's name, once you're happy with the changes, click on **"Save"**.

![Change name](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-change-template-name.png)

Alternatively, you can rename a [template](/products/pro/documenting/templates/) from the **Edit view** by simply clicking the name of your template at the top of the screen and then selecting the **Rename** option

![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-rename-template-from-edit-view.png)
