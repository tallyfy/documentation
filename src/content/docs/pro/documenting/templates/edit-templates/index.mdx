## Options for editing templates

You can edit any template in your Library in multiple ways. Only Standard and Admin members can edit templates, and Light members cannot.

:::note[Role permission requirement]
[Members](/products/pro/documenting/members/) with a "Light" role cannot create or edit [templates](/products/pro/documenting/templates/). Only members with a "Standard" role (if they have appropriate permissions) or "Administrator" role can edit [templates](/products/pro/documenting/templates/).
:::

To begin your changes and edits - head over to your [template](/products/pro/documenting/templates/) and click on the "edit button".

![Click edit](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-open-edit-mode-template.png)

This is what we call the **Edit view**, we'll be using this interface to make all of our changes.

![Edit view](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-show-template-created-by-tallyfyai.png)
