## How to add guidance text to a step in a template

Tallyfy allows you to add optional descriptions, instructions, or examples that appear under each [form field](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/what-are-form-fields-in-tallyfy/) in a [task](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/). This feature provides clear directions to your team when filling out form fields.

To add guidance text:

1. Open the [template](/products/pro/documenting/templates/) step you want to add guidance text to.
2. Go to the **forms** tab.
3. Select the form field you wish to add guidance text to.
4. Click on the **Advanced** section.
5. Type in your guidance text.
6. Save your changes.

![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-add-guidance-text.png)

When the [process](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/processes/) starts, the [task](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/) will display the guidance text in italics under the form field title. Any URLs included in the guidance text will be clickable, allowing team members to access additional resources.
