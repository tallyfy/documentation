## Using Tags in Tallyfy

Tags help organize and filter templates and processes. This article explains how to create, apply, and use tags effectively.

## Manage Tags in Your Organization

Centrally manage tags through organization settings:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Organization > Tags**
2. To edit a tag, click on it to rename or change its color
   ![Edit tag](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-edit-tag.png)
3. To delete a tag, click the â‹® icon and select **Delete Tag**

## Adding Tags to Templates

Add tags to templates for better organization:

1. Open the template in **Edit** mode
2. Click **Settings > Summary**
3. Enter a tag in the **Tag** field
4. Click **Save Changes**

![Add tag to template](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-add-tag-to-template.png)

## Adding Tags to Processes

Tag processes for filtering and categorization:

1. Open the desired process
2. Click the **Add Tag** button

![Add tag to process](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-add-tag-process.png)

## Filtering Processes by Tags

Use tags to find specific processes:

1. Go to the **Tracker** section
2. Click **Add filter**
   ![Click filter](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-click-filter.png)
3. Select the desired **Tags** from the dropdown menu
   ![Filter by tag](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-filter-process-by-tag.png)
