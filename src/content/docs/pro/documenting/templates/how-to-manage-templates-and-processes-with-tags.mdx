## Everything You Need to Know About Tags in Tallyfy

**Simplify and Transform Markdown: Help Article for Tallyfy**

Welcome to Tallyfy, the ultimate organizational tool! In this article, we will show you how to use tags to better manage your templates and processes. Let's get started!

## Manage Tags in Your Organization

1. Add, edit, and remove tags in your organization via **Settings > Organization > Tags**.
2. Rename a tag or change its color by clicking on it.
   ![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-edit-tag.png)
3. To delete tags, click on â‹® and select **Delete Tag**.

## Adding Tags to Templates

1. Open your desired template in **Edit** mode, and click on **Settings > Summary**.

2. Add a new tag in the **Tag** field and click **Save Changes**.

![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-add-tag-to-template.png)

## Adding Tags to Processes

1. Open your desired process.
2. Select the **Add Tag** button.

![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-add-tag-process.png)

## Filtering Processes by Tags

1. Go to the **Tracker** section and click on the **Add filter** option.
   ![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-click-filter.png)
2. Select the **Tags** you wish to see.
   ![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-filter-process-by-tag.png)
