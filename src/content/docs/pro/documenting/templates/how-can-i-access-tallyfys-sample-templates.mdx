## How to access and use sample templates

Tallyfy provides pre-built [template](/products/pro/documenting/templates/) libraries to jumpstart your [process](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/processes/) implementation with industry-standard workflows that can be customized to your specific needs.

### Benefits of sample Templates
- **Quick start** - Implement processes without building from scratch
- **Best practices** - Leverage professionally designed workflow patterns
- **Standardization** - Ensure consistent process design across your organization
- **Learning tool** - Understand template construction through real examples
- **Time savings** - Deploy common [processes](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/processes/) in minutes instead of hours

### Accessing sample Templates

Tallyfy's sample templates are available through multiple channels:

1. **Public Template Library**
   - Visit [Tallyfy's Public Library](https://go.tallyfy.com/public/library)
   - Browse templates by category
   - Preview templates before importing
   - Import directly to your organization

2. **Within Your Tallyfy Account**
   - Navigate to **Templates** section
   - Click **Import** or **New Template** 
   - Select **Sample Templates** from the options
   - Choose from the available template categories

### Customizing sample Templates

After importing a sample template:

<Steps>
1. Review the template structure and flow
2. Modify step titles and descriptions to match your terminology
3. Adjust assignments to reflect your team structure
4. Add or remove steps as needed for your specific requirements
5. Update [form fields](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/what-are-form-fields-in-tallyfy/) to capture your required information
6. Modify deadlines to reflect your timeline expectations
7. Test the customized template with a sample [process](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/processes/)
</Steps>

### Template organization best practices

For effective template management:
- Group templates by department or function
- Use consistent naming conventions
- Add descriptive tags for easier filtering
- Create folders for different process categories
- Document customizations made to sample templates
