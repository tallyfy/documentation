## What sample templates we offer

Welcome to Tallyfy! So you've signed up and now you want to start building your Template Library. Well, guess what? We've got some awesome sample templates ready and waiting for you!

To access these sample templates, just visit our Public Library [here](https://go.tallyfy.com/public/library). You'll find a ton of useful templates to get you started.

Here's some ideas on folders you can create for your organization and the types of templates you might like to put in them.

## Folder: Marketing

- New Email Campaign
- Facebook Ad Creation
- Social Media Posting

## Folder: HR

- Employee Onboarding
- Vacation Request

## Folder: Product

## Folder: General/Admin

- Plan Regular Events
- Daily/Weekly Tasks
- Weekly/Monthly Reporting

## Folder: Finance/Accounts

- Expense Claim Request

## Folder: Sales
