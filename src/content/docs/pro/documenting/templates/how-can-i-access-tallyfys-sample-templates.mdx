## Sample templates in Tallyfy

Tallyfy provides pre-built templates to help you quickly establish process workflows. Access these templates through our [Public Library](https://go.tallyfy.com/public/library).

Below is a suggested organizational structure for templates by department or function. This structure can help you organize your own templates efficiently.
