## Using Tallyfy's sample templates

Import ready-made templates from Tallyfy's public library in a few clicks. They cover everything from employee onboarding to sales processes. Instead of starting from a blank template, you'll get proven workflows to customize for your team.

### Why use sample templates

- **Faster setup** - launch your first process in minutes, not hours
- **Learn by example** - see how successful companies structure their workflows
- **Stay consistent** - every department uses the same proven process structure

### Finding sample templates

Visit [Tallyfy's Public Library](https://go.tallyfy.com/public/library) to browse templates. You can filter by type, industry, and topic. Preview any template before importing, then import it to your account with one click.

### Customizing sample templates

After importing a template, make it yours:

<Steps>
1. Review the workflow - does the flow make sense for your team?
2. Rename steps to match your company's language
3. Fix the assignments - replace generic roles with your actual people
4. Add or cut steps as needed
5. Update the [form fields](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/what-are-form-fields-in-tallyfy/) to capture your specific data
6. Adjust deadlines to match your team's pace
7. Run a test - [launch](/products/pro/launching/) a dummy [process](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/processes/) before going live
</Steps>

### Organizing imported templates

- Create folders by department or process type
- Name templates clearly - "Employee Onboarding v2" beats "HR Process 3"
- Tag templates so they're easy to find later
- Document major changes with notes like "Added legal review step for California compliance"
