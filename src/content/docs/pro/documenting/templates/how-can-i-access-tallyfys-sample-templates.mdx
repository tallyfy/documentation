## How can I access and use Tallyfy's sample templates?

Tallyfy provides comprehensive libraries of ready-made template examples designed to accelerate workflow implementation across common business scenarios.

These professionally crafted templates enable rapid setup of standard processes based on industry best practices, providing excellent starting points that can be customized to match your specific organizational requirements and operational preferences.

Using sample templates significantly reduces implementation time while ensuring your workflows follow proven structural patterns.

### What benefits do sample templates provide for workflow development?

Sample templates deliver significant advantages for workflow implementation and organizational efficiency:

- **Accelerated Implementation**: Establish functional processes rapidly without starting from blank templates or reinventing common workflows
- **Professional Examples**: Learn from expertly designed workflow examples demonstrating industry best practices and proven structures
- **Organizational Consistency**: Maintain standardized process approaches across departments and teams for uniform quality
- **Template Education**: Understand template construction through real-world examples and proven workflow structures
- **Time Efficiency**: Deploy common processes significantly faster than custom development while maintaining professional quality

These benefits enable organizations to quickly establish robust workflow systems while learning effective template design principles.

### Where can I find and access sample templates?

You can find Tallyfy's sample templates in two convenient locations:

1. **Tallyfy's Public Template Library**
   - Go to [Tallyfy's Public Library](https://go.tallyfy.com/public/library) for comprehensive browsing.
   - Look through templates organized by category (like HR, Marketing, etc.) for easy navigation.
   - Preview any template before you decide to use it to ensure it meets your needs.
   - Click to import a template directly into your Tallyfy account for immediate use.

2. **Inside Your Tallyfy Account**
   - Go to the Templates section in Tallyfy for integrated access.
   - Click Import or New Template to access creation options.
   - Choose the Sample Templates option for pre-built workflows.
   - Browse the available categories and choose a template that fits your requirements.

Both access methods provide the same comprehensive template library with different user experiences.

### How can I customize a sample template for my specific organizational needs?

Once you import a sample template into your account:

<Steps>
1. Look through the steps and overall flow.
2. Change step names and descriptions to use your company's wording.
3. Update who is assigned to tasks to match your team.
4. Add or remove steps if needed.
5. Change the [form fields](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/what-are-form-fields-in-tallyfy/) to collect the information you need.
6. Adjust deadlines based on how long things should take for you.
7. Test the template by [launching](/products/pro/launching/) a test [process](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/processes/).
</Steps>

### What are best practices for organizing imported sample templates?

- Put templates into folders based on department or type of process for logical grouping.
- Use clear and consistent names for your templates that reflect their purpose and scope.
- Add tags to make templates easier to find and filter when your library grows.
- Write down any important changes you made to sample templates so others understand customizations.

These practices ensure your template library remains organized and accessible as you import and customize multiple sample templates.
