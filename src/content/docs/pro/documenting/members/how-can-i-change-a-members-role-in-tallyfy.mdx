## How can you change the role of a member on your organization

In this article, we will learn about the different roles of members in Tallyfy and how to change them.

To change a member's role, follow these steps:

1. Go to your _Settings_ and click on _Organization_. Then, select _Members_.
2. Find the profile name of the member you want to change: ![image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-select-member.png)
3. Choose the appropriate role from the available options: ![image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-change-user-permissions.png)
4. Don't forget to save your changes!

That's it! You now know how to change a member's role in Tallyfy.
