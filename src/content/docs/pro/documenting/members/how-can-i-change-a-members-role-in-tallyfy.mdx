## Changing a member's role

### Prerequisites
- Administrator access
- At least one member whose role you want to change

### Available member roles

Tallyfy offers three distinct member roles:

- **Administrator**: Full access to all features, settings, and permissions
- **Standard**: Customizable permissions set by administrators
- **Light**: Limited access with basic template viewing and task completion capabilities

### Steps to change a role

1. Navigate to **Settings > Organization > Members**
2. Click on the profile name of the member to modify
   ![Select member interface](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-select-member.png)
3. Select the desired role from the dropdown menu
   ![Change role interface](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-change-user-permissions.png)
4. Click **Save** to apply the changes

### When to change roles

- Promote a Standard member to Administrator when they need organization-wide control
- Change to Light role for occasional users who only need to complete assigned tasks
- Adjust roles as team responsibilities evolve
