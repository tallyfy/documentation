## Member role comparison

Tallyfy has two primary [member roles](/products/pro/documenting/members/) with different permission levels and capabilities: Administrator and Standard.

### Administrator members

Administrator members have complete control over the Tallyfy platform and can perform all actions available in the system.

#### Administrator capabilities
- **Organization management:**
  - [Invite new members](/products/pro/documenting/members/how-to-invite-and-activate-members-on-tallyfy/)
  - Edit [billing](/products/pro/settings/billing/) information
  - Update plan subscriptions
  - View/modify [organization details](/products/pro/settings/org-settings/how-can-i-update-my-organization-details/)
  - Access all [templates](/products/pro/documenting/templates/) and [processes](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/processes/)
  
- **Member management:**
  - Set permissions for all members
  - Edit member profiles
  - Remove members
  - View activity logs
  
- **Template/process control:**
  - Create, edit, and view all [templates](/products/pro/documenting/templates/)
  - Set template and [process](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/processes/) access permissions
  - Delete archived templates
  - Manage all aspects of process execution

#### Finding administrators
To view all administrator members:
1. Navigate to **Settings > Organization > Members**
2. Look for members with the **Administrator** role

### Standard members

Standard members have limited permissions focused on day-to-day operational [tasks](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/) rather than administrative functions.

#### Standard member capabilities
- **Template management:**
  - Create, edit, and view permitted [templates](/products/pro/documenting/templates/)
  - Organize templates into folders
  - Import templates (if permitted)
  
- **Organization interaction:**
  - [Invite](/products/pro/documenting/members/how-to-invite-and-activate-members-on-tallyfy/) other standard members (if permitted)
  - View organization details like members list and [guests](/products/pro/documenting/guests/)
  - Access tags, custom branding, and [snippets](/products/pro/documenting/templates/snippets/)
  - View [tasks](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/) and email logs of other members
  
- **System access:**
  - View [API](/products/pro/integrations/open-api/) Access Token
  - Update custom branding settings and logo

#### Standard member restrictions
Standard members cannot:
- Set template and [process](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/processes/) access permissions
- Delete archived templates
- Access organization and member profiles
- View plan and [billing](/products/pro/settings/billing/) information
- Modify subscription details
