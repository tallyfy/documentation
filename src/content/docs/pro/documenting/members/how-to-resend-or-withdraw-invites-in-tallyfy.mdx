## How to resend or withdraw invites

Sending and withdrawing invites on Tallyfy is a breeze! If your invite gets lost in the mail or you accidentally invited the wrong person, don't worry. We've got you covered.

To resend or withdraw an invite, simply follow these steps:

1. Go to the sidebar and click on **Settings**.
2. From there, click on **Organization** and select **Members**.
3. In the member list, Scroll horizontally until you see an "Action" column.
4. Find the member with the pending invitation and choose whether to **Resend** or **Withdraw** the invite.

That's it! You're all set to manage your invites effortlessly.
