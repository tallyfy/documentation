## Inviting and activating members

### Prerequisites
- Administrator access to invite members
- Available member seats in your plan
- Email addresses for the members you want to invite

### Inviting new members

1. Access the invitation screen using one of these methods:
   - Click **Invite+** in the sidebar
   - Select **Invite+** from the dropdown menu next to your name
   - Navigate to **Settings > Organization > Members** and click **Invite+**
   - Click **+Create** in the sidebar and select **Invite Member**

   ![Invite button location](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-invite-member-sidebar.png)

2. Enter the required information:
   - Email address of the invitee
   - First and last name
   - Role (Administrator or Standard)
   - Optional welcome message

   ![Invitation form](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-invite-member-menu.png)

3. Click **Send Invitation**

### Managing pending invitations
- View all pending invitations under **Settings > Organization > Members**
- Resend invitations if needed
- Cancel invitations by selecting **Withdraw**

### Activating a member account (for invitees)

1. Open the invitation email
2. Click the **Join Now** button in the email
3. Create a password for your account
   ![Set password screen](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/file-IH8Zof4MfQ.png)
4. Complete any additional profile setup steps

### Verifying activation status
Administrators can confirm activated members by:
1. Navigate to **Settings > Organization > Members**
2. Check that the member's status shows as **Active**
