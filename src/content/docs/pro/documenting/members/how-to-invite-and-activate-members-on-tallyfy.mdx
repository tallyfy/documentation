## How to invite and activate members

### What you need (Administrators)
- Administrator permission level.
- An available paid [member](/products/pro/documenting/members/) seat in your company's plan.
- The email addresses of the people you want to invite.

### Inviting new Members (as Administrator)

<Steps>
1. Start an invitation. You can do this in several ways:
    - Click **Invite+** in the left sidebar.
    - Click your name in the top right, then choose **Invite+**.
    - Go to **Settings** > **Organization** > **Members** and click **Invite+**.
    - Click **+Create** in the top bar, then choose **Invite Member**.
   ![Invite button location](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-invite-member-sidebar.png)
2. Type the email address of the person you're inviting.
3. Enter their first and last name.
4. Choose their starting [Role](/products/pro/documenting/members/how-do-i-manage-member-permissions-in-tallyfy/) (usually Administrator or Standard).
5. *(Optional)* Add a personal welcome message.
   ![Invitation form](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-invite-member-menu.png)
6. Click **Send Invitation**.
</Steps>

### Managing invitations you've sent (as Administrator)
- You can see everyone you've invited who hasn't joined yet under **Settings** > **Organization** > **Members** (look for "Invited" status).
- If someone didn't get the email, you can resend the invitation from there.
- If you invited the wrong person, you can cancel the invitation by clicking **Withdraw**.

### How invited people activate their account

This is what the person you invited needs to do:
<Steps>
1. Open the invitation email from Tallyfy.
2. Click the **Join Now** button inside the email.
3. Create a secure password for their new Tallyfy account.
   ![Set password screen](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/file-IH8Zof4MfQ.png)
4. Follow any other steps shown to finish setting up their profile.
</Steps>

### Checking if a member is active (as Administrator)
Administrators can see if someone has successfully activated their account:
<Steps>
1. Go to **Settings** > **Organization** > **Members**.
2. Find the person in the list and check that their status says **Active**.
</Steps>
