## How can you convert a guest into a member in your organization

Sometimes, you may need to convert a guest into a member in your organization. This can be done easily in Tallyfy. Here's how:

## Convert a guest into a member

There are a few steps to follow in order to successfully convert a guest into a member:

1. Go to your _Settings_ and click on _Organization_. Then, select _Guests_.
2. Find the profile name of the guest you want to convert and click on the _Convert to member_ option.
3. This will automatically add the guest as a member to your organization.

That's it! You have successfully converted a guest into a member in Tallyfy.
