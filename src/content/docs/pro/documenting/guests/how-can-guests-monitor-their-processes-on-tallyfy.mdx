## The guest dashboard

The [guest](/products/pro/documenting/guests/) dashboard provides external users with a centralized view of all [tasks](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/) assigned to them across multiple [processes](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/processes/).

### Accessing the guest dashboard

[Guests](/products/pro/documenting/guests/) can access their dashboard in two ways:
1. Through the **unique [task link](/products/pro/documenting/guests/how-to-find-a-guest-task-link-in-tallyfy/)** sent via email
2. By visiting [https://go.tallyfy.com](https://go.tallyfy.com/) and entering their email address

### Dashboard features

The guest dashboard includes:

- **Process list** - All processes containing tasks assigned to the guest
- **Task cards** - Individual tasks requiring the guest's attention
- **Status indicators** - Visual cues showing the status of each task (pending, in progress, completed)
- **Comment section** - Area to provide feedback or ask questions about tasks
- **Attachment viewer** - Access to files shared as part of tasks
- **Due date indicators** - Time remaining to complete tasks

### Navigating the dashboard

#### Viewing process details
1. Locate the [process](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/processes/) card in the dashboard
2. Click on the process name to expand details
3. View all [tasks](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/) within that process assigned to you

#### Working with tasks
1. Select the [task](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/) you want to view or complete
2. Review the task description and requirements
3. Complete any [form fields](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/what-are-form-fields-in-tallyfy/) or uploads requested
4. Add comments if needed
5. Click **Complete** when finished

#### Tracking progress
- Completed tasks display a checkmark
- Current tasks are highlighted
- Upcoming tasks show when they will become active
- Process completion percentage indicates overall progress

### Guest permissions

As a [guest](/products/pro/documenting/guests/) user, you can:
- View and complete your assigned [tasks](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/)
- Access task-related documents
- Add comments on your tasks
- Track progress of [processes](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/processes/) where you're involved
- Receive email notifications about tasks
