## Learn ways to organize your processes

Tallyfy provides ways to categorize and classify active [processes](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/processes/) by applying tags or adding them to folders.

To see what matters most, you can also filter and customize views like the **Tasks** and **Tracker** views in the left navigation bar.
