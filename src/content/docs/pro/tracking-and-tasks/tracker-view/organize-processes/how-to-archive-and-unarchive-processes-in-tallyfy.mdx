## How to archive and unarchive processes

In this article, we will learn how to archive and unarchive processes in Tallyfy. Archiving a process is a convenient way to remove it from your active view without deleting it permanently.

Important note - any member in _Light_ role cannot archive or unarchive a process.

## Archive a Process

To archive a process, follow these simple steps:

1. Navigate to the _Tracker_ view from the sidebar.
2. Find the process you want to archive and click on the three-dot menu icon (â‹®) next to it.
3. Select _Archive_ from the menu.
   ![Screenshot](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-archive-process.png)

## Unarchive a Process

To unarchive a process, follow these simple steps:

1. Go to the _Tracker_ view.
2. Filter processes by **Archived**
   ![Screenshot](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-filter-archived-processes.png)

3. You will see a list of all your archived processes. Click on _Unarchive_ next to the process you want to restore.
