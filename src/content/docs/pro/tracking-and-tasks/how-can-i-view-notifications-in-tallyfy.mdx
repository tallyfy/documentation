## Accessing notifications in Tallyfy

Notifications keep you informed about important actions and updates in your workflow processes. They appear in real-time and provide an easy way to track changes and required actions.

## Viewing current notifications

Notifications appear in the top-right corner of the Tallyfy interface:

![Notification indicator](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-show-notifications.png)

## Viewing all notifications

To access your complete notification history:

1. Click the notification icon in the top-right corner
2. Select **VIEW ALL NOTIFICATIONS**

This opens a comprehensive list of all notifications you've received:

![All notifications](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-show-all-notifications.png)
