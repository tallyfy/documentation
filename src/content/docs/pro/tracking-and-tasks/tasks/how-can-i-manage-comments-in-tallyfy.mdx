## How to add, edit, and delete comments on tasks

Comments allow you and your team to discuss specific [tasks](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/) directly within Tallyfy.

### Adding a comment

<Steps>
1. Open the task you want to comment on.
2. Scroll down to the **Comments** section at the bottom.
3. Type your message in the comment box.
4. Click **Post**.
5. Your comment appears in the list with your name and the time.
</Steps>

### Editing your own comments

If you need to fix a typo or change something you wrote:

<Steps>
1. Find your comment in the list.
2. Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon ‚úèÔ∏è) on your comment.
3. Make your changes.
4. Click **Save**.
5. Your comment will now show "(edited)" next to the timestamp.
</Steps>

### Deleting your comments

If you want to remove a comment you posted:

<Steps>
1. Find your comment.
2. Click the **Delete** button (trash can icon üóëÔ∏è) on your comment.
3. Confirm you want to delete it.
4. The comment will be permanently removed.
</Steps>

### Getting Notified (@Mentions)

To make sure a specific person sees your comment, type `@` followed by their name (e.g., `@Jane Doe`). Select them from the list that appears. They will get a notification about your comment.

### Tips for Good Comments

*   Keep comments focused on the specific task.
*   Use @mentions to direct questions or updates to the right people.
*   Use formatting (like **bold** or bullet points) to make comments clear.
