## How to add, edit, and delete comments on tasks

Comments let you and your team discuss specific [tasks](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/) directly within Tallyfy.

### Adding a comment

<Steps>
1. Open the task you want to comment on.
2. Scroll down to the **Comments** section.
3. Type your message.
4. Click **Post**.
5. Your comment appears with your name and the time.
</Steps>

### Editing your own comments

To fix a typo or change what you wrote:

<Steps>
1. Find your comment.
2. Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon ‚úèÔ∏è) on your comment.
3. Edit your comment.
4. Click **Save**.
5. Your comment will show "(edited)" next to the timestamp.
</Steps>

### Deleting your comments

To remove a comment you posted:

<Steps>
1. Find your comment.
2. Click the **Delete** button (trash can icon üóëÔ∏è) on your comment.
3. Confirm the deletion.
4. The comment will be permanently deleted.
</Steps>

### Getting notified (@mentions)

To ensure a specific person sees your comment, type `@` and start typing their name (e.g., `@Jane Doe`). Select their name from the list that appears. They'll get a notification about your comment.

### Tips for good comments

*   Keep comments task-focused.
*   Use `@mentions` to direct questions to the right people.
*   Use formatting (like **bold** or lists) to make them clear.
