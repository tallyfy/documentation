## How to add, edit, and delete comments in tasks

Comments in Tallyfy facilitate team collaboration by enabling discussions directly within tasks and processes. This guide explains how to create, edit, and delete comments effectively.

### Adding comments

<Steps>
1. Navigate to the task or process where you want to add a comment
2. Scroll down to the **Comments** section at the bottom of the page
3. Type your message in the comment field
4. Click **Post** to publish your comment
5. Your comment will appear in the comments feed with your name and timestamp
</Steps>

### Editing comments

You can modify your own comments after posting:

<Steps>
1. Locate the comment you want to edit in the comments feed
2. Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon) in the top-right corner of your comment
3. Make your desired changes in the editing field
4. Click **Save** to update your comment
5. Your edited comment will display an "(edited)" indicator
</Steps>

### Deleting comments

If a comment is no longer relevant:

<Steps>
1. Find the comment you want to remove
2. Click the **Delete** button (trash icon) in the top-right corner of the comment
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted
4. The comment will be permanently removed from the comments feed
</Steps>

### Comment notifications

When you're mentioned in a comment or someone comments on a task assigned to you:
- You'll receive an email notification (if enabled in your notification settings)
- The comment will appear in your Tallyfy notifications

### Best practices for commenting

- Keep comments concise and relevant to the task
- Use @mentions to notify specific team members
- Include links to relevant resources when applicable
- Use formatting options for clarity (bold, italics, bullet points)
- Reply directly to existing comments to maintain conversation threads
