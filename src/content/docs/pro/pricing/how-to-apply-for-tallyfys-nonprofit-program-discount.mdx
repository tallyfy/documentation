## Applying for the non-profit discount

We offer a **20% lifetime discount** for Tallyfy Pro for annual plans only, to qualifying organizations.

## Who qualifies?

We welcome applications from organizations that have a valid charitable status with the IRS (in the United States) or a local tax service/charity commission.

Your organization cannot be any of the following:

- A legislative or political activity organization
- A church, association of churches, or other religious organization
- A school, college, or a related organization.
- A government office
- An organization that attempts to influence public opinion
- A hospital, organization involved with health insurance or group health plans, or a related organization
- A private grant-making, independent, or operating foundation

Additionally, your organization cannot do any of the following:

- Discriminate on various grounds
- Promote a specific religious affiliation as part of its mission or services (affiliation is okay)

If you are uncertain about your qualification status, contact your Tallyfy representative or the support team for an evaluation. Qualifications are assessed on a case-by-case basis.

## How to apply

If you qualify:

<Steps>
1. If your organization is not using Tallyfy yet, sign up at [tallyfy.com/start/](https://tallyfy.com/start/) and provide your Tallyfy organization ID, which can be found [here](/products/pro/miscellaneous/how-to-find-your-tallyfy-ids/).

2. Contact our support department - providing the following information:

- Your organization's legal name
- Tallyfy account owner/bill payer's name, email, and contact number
- Organization's address
- Organization's website
- A brief description of what your organization is all about
- A valid certificate/evidence of non-profit/charity status
</Steps>

If your application is approved, we will provide you with a coupon code that you can use when upgrading to a paid annual plan.

## Important note

- Please note that we do not offer refunds for subscription fees paid before approval for the Tallyfy for non-profits program.
- While Tallyfy can be used by everyone, not every organization may meet our eligibility requirements for this program. We determine eligibility at our sole discretion, and we reserve the right to grant or deny applications at any time, for any reason. We may also update our eligibility guidelines as needed.
