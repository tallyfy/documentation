## What is Tallyfy's nonprofit discount?

Tallyfy offers a permanent discount for qualifying nonprofit and charitable organizations. Contact Tallyfy for current rates.

## Who's eligible?

Your organization needs official charitable status from a government tax authority - like IRS 501(c)(3) in the US or equivalent international certification.

These categories are **ineligible**:

*   Political advocacy and lobbying organizations
*   Religious institutions and congregations
*   Educational institutions
*   Government agencies
*   Public opinion research organizations
*   Healthcare facilities and medical insurance providers
*   Private foundations

Organizations that practice discrimination or require specific religious affiliations for service access are also ineligible.

Not sure if you qualify? Contact support before applying.

## How to apply

<Steps>
1. Create a Tallyfy account at [tallyfy.com/start/](https://tallyfy.com/start/). Find your [Organization ID](/products/pro/miscellaneous/how-to-find-your-tallyfy-ids/) - you'll need it.
2. Contact Tallyfy support (in-app help or email `support@tallyfy.com`).
3. Provide the following:
    * Legal organization name
    * Admin contact details - name, email, and phone number
    * Official mailing address
    * Organization website URL
    * Brief mission statement
    * Nonprofit status documentation (determination letter, registration certificate, or equivalent)
</Steps>

Tallyfy reviews each application. Once approved, the discount gets applied to your subscription.

## Terms and conditions

*   Tallyfy determines discount eligibility.
*   The discount applies permanently to annual subscriptions.
*   Discounts can't be applied retroactively.
*   Tallyfy reserves the right to approve or deny applications and to modify program criteria at any time.
