You build and run the workflows everyone else uses. A full seat means you can create and edit templates, automate them, launch and track processes, and (if you're an administrator) manage people, billing, and settings. Here's the whole job, grouped by what you're trying to do.

- **[Invite your team](/products/pro/documenting/members/how-to-invite-and-activate-members-on-tallyfy/)** Add members and pick their roles.

## Build templates

- **[Create your first template](/products/pro/tutorials/your-first-template/)** From scratch, from a document, or with Tallyfy AI.

- **[Edit a template](/products/pro/documenting/templates/edit-templates/)** Add steps, descriptions, and settings.
- **[Assign steps to people](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/edit-task/how-can-i-assign-members-to-tasks-in-tallyfy/)** By person, group, or job title.
