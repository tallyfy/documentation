### Changes

- **[UI]** Guest invitations during process launch now work more smoothly - just type an email and press Enter, no extra clicking needed.
- **[UI]** Templates Activity Feed now shows all activity for better visibility, with filtering options coming soon to help you focus on what matters most.

### Bug Fixes

- **[UI]** Fixed at-mentions in one-off tasks so clicking notifications now properly opens the task for mentioned users who aren't assigned to it.
- **[UI]** Fixed table form fields now properly display values and trigger automations, making your workflow data more reliable and consistent.
- **[UI]** Fixed button alignment and labeling in Tracker view so Approve/Reject buttons display consistently and Email Draft tasks show the correct "Complete" or "Send" labels.
- **[UI]** Fixed horizontal scrolling and improved filter memory in Tasks view so your sort and status selections stay put when you navigate away and come back.
- **[UI]** Fixed button text getting cut off in Spanish, German, and French by making buttons automatically resize to fit longer translations.
