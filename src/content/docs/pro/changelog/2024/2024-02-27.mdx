### New Features

- **[UI]** You can now set Light role as the default for new SSO members, helping you save costs on user licensing.
- **[UI]** Organizations can now add Light users at a lower cost - these users can view and complete tasks but cannot create or edit templates, making it more affordable to include your entire team.
- **[UI]** You can now choose your preferred date format in My Account > Personalization, with 122 options that apply consistently across all tasks, deadlines, and forms.
- **[UI]** Added milestones to organize your process steps into collapsible sections, making complex workflows easier to navigate and manage.

### Changes

- **[UI]** Milestones now appear as section headers when viewing procedures, making it easier to understand process structure at a glance.
- **[UI]** "Power Tools" renamed to "Tools" in templates and approvals for a cleaner interface and easier future updates.
- **[UI]** Fixed confusing task linking behavior so you can now clearly add design notes to templates and operational tasks to active processes.
- **[UI]** Kept invite modal form styling consistent to ensure green/red input validation remains clear and intuitive.
- **[UI]** We're building a table view for procedure templates that includes CSV export, so you won't need to create dummy processes just to view form fields in spreadsheet format.
- **[UI]** Simplified user roles by adding a dedicated "Light" role option, making it easier to manage permissions and billing for team members.
- **[UI]** Power Tools now shows assignment details when bulk-changing assignees, making it easier to spot tasks that need guest assignments.
- **[UI]** AI-powered template creation now responds faster, reducing wait times when generating new procedure templates from your descriptions.
- **[UI]** Template icons now show meaningful status and type information, making it easier to identify ready-to-use templates at a glance.
- **[UI]** You can now easily assign team members to job titles in active processes using the new bulk assignment tool, plus we fixed the broken single assignment button.
