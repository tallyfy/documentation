## Tutorial: Creating Your First Template

This tutorial walks you through creating your first process [template](/products/pro/documenting/templates/). Templates are the blueprints for your repeatable workflows.

### Creating a Template (Using Tallyfy AI Example)

<Steps>
1.  Click the **+ Create** button (top left sidebar).
    ![Create button](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-click-create-fullscreen.png)
2.  Select **Create Template** from the menu.
    ![Create Template option](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-click-create-template-from-button.png)
3.  Choose how you want to create the template. For this example, let's use **Tallyfy AI**. (You can also choose **From scratch** or **From SOP document**).
4.  Let's ask the AI to create a 'Client Onboarding' process for an accounting company.
    *   Select **Procedure** as the template type.
    *   Type a descriptive name like "Client Onboarding for Accounting Firm".
    *   *(Optional)* Add your company type (Accounting) to help the AI.
    *   Click **Create**.
        ![Creating a template with Tallyfy AI](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-create-client-onboarding-template-from-tallyfyAI.png)
5.  Tallyfy AI will generate a list of suggested steps. Review them. You can delete or change them later. Click **Continue**.
    ![Reviewing AI-generated steps](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-click-create-template-by-tallyfyai.png)
6.  Your template is created! You are now in the template editor where you can make further changes or [launch your first process](/products/pro/tutorials/your-first-process/).
    ![Template editor view](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-show-template-created-by-tallyfyai.png)
</Steps>

### Other Ways to Create Templates

Remember, besides using Tallyfy AI, you can also:

*   **Start From Scratch**: Build the template step-by-step yourself.
*   **Upload a Document/Flowchart**: Upload an existing Word doc, PDF, or flowchart, and Tallyfy AI will try to convert it into a template.

See [Create a Template](/products/pro/documenting/templates/create-template/) for more details on all creation methods.
