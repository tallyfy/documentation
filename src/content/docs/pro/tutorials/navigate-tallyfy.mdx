## Navigating the Tallyfy platform

Tallyfy uses a few key areas to help you find information and manage your work effectively.

### Left sidebar menu

This is your primary navigation tool, located on the left side of the screen.

![Tallyfy left navigation bar with numbered elements](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-sidebar-numerated.png)

1.  **+ CREATE**: Start new items like processes, templates, or tasks.
2.  **HOME**: Go to your main dashboard view.
3.  **TASKS**: See your personal task list ([learn more](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/)).
4.  **TRACKER**: View all active processes ([learn more](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tracker-view/)).
5.  **TEMPLATES**: Access your library of process templates ([learn more](/products/pro/documenting/templates/)).
6.  **PEOPLE**: Manage team members (Administrators only).
7.  **INVITE+**: Invite new members to join your organization.
8.  **GUIDE**: Open the Tallyfy help documentation.
9.  **SUPPORT**: Contact the Tallyfy support team.
10. **SETTINGS**: Access your personal and organization settings.

:::note[Visibility depends on your role]
Some sections, like **People** or certain **Settings**, are only visible if you have the Administrator role. Standard and Light roles have different access levels.
:::

### Universal search bar

![Tallyfy search bar showing results](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-search-bar-results.png)

The search bar at the top allows you to find items across your entire Tallyfy account, including processes, templates, tasks, snippets, and members. Just type keywords to search.

*   **Sharing Search Results**: Click the share icon next to any search result to copy a direct link to that item.
    ![Copy search result link icon](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-copy-search-result-link.png)

### Top bar icons on the right side

*   **Language Selector** (Globe icon üåê): Click to change the display language for Tallyfy menus and buttons. If your organization uses the [Azure Translation integration](/products/pro/integrations/azure-translation/), you can also select a language for automatic content translation here.
    ![Language selection menu](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-change-language.png)
*   **Notifications** (Bell icon üîî): Click to view recent notifications about task assignments, comments, and other activities. See [Check Notifications](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/how-can-i-view-notifications-in-tallyfy/).
