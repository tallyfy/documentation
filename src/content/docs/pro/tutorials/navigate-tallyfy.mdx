## Navigating the Tallyfy Platform

Tallyfy has a few main areas to help you find your way around and manage your work.

### Left Sidebar Menu

This is your main navigation menu on the left side of the screen.

![Tallyfy left navigation bar with numbered elements](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-sidebar-numerated.png)

1.  **+ CREATE**: Start new items like processes, templates, or tasks.
2.  **HOME**: Your main dashboard view.
3.  **TASKS**: See your individual task list ([learn more](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/)).
4.  **TRACKER**: View all running processes ([learn more](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tracker-view/)).
5.  **TEMPLATES**: Access your library of process templates ([learn more](/products/pro/documenting/templates/)).
6.  **PEOPLE**: Manage team members (Admins).
7.  **INVITE+**: Invite new members to your organization.
8.  **GUIDE**: Open the help documentation.
9.  **SUPPORT**: Contact the Tallyfy support team.
10. **SETTINGS**: Access personal and organization settings.

:::note[What You See Depends on Your Role]
Some sections (like People or parts of Settings) might only be visible if you have the Administrator role. Standard and Light roles have different levels of access.
:::

### Universal Search Bar

![Tallyfy search bar showing results](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-search-bar-results.png)

The search bar at the very top lets you search across your entire Tallyfy account for processes, templates, tasks, snippets, and members. Just type in keywords.

*   **Sharing Search Results**: Click the share icon next to a result to copy a direct link to it.
    ![Copy search result link icon](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-copy-search-result-link.png)

### Top Bar Icons (Right Side)

*   **Language Selector** (Globe icon üåê): Click this to change the language Tallyfy displays its menus and buttons in. You can also choose a language for automatic content translation if your organization has set up the [Azure Translation](/products/pro/integrations/azure-translation/) feature.
    ![Language selection menu](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-change-language.png)
*   **Notifications** (Bell icon üîî): Click this to see your recent notifications about task assignments, comments, etc. See [Check Notifications](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/how-can-i-view-notifications-in-tallyfy/).
