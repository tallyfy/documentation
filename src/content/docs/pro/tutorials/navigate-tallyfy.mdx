## Platform Navigation

Tallyfy provides an intuitive user interface designed for efficient workflow management. This guide explains the primary navigation elements.

### Left Navigation Bar

![Tallyfy left navigation bar with numbered elements](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-sidebar-numerated.png)

1. **CREATE** - Create new processes, templates, and tasks
2. **HOME** - View your dashboard with all processes, templates, and tasks
3. **TASKS** - Manage tasks assigned to you and create one-off tasks ([Learn more about tasks](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tasks/))
4. **TRACKER** - View and manage all running processes in your organization ([Learn more about tracking processes](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/tracker-view/))
5. **TEMPLATES** - Access the library of templates in your organization ([Learn more about templates](/products/pro/documenting/templates/))
6. **PEOPLE** - Manage organization members
7. **INVITE** - Invite new members to your organization
8. **GUIDE** - Access product documentation
9. **SUPPORT** - Submit tickets to the technical and product team
10. **SETTINGS** - Manage organization settings, billing, and configurations

:::note[Member Permissions]
Access to specific sections depends on your role within the organization. Contact your organization administrator if you need access to restricted sections. Learn more about member permissions in the [standard and admin members article](/products/pro/documenting/members/standard-admin-members/).
:::

### Search Functionality

![Tallyfy search bar with results](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-search-bar-results.png)

The search bar at the top of the screen allows you to search for processes, templates, tasks, or members within your organization. Enter keywords to see relevant results organized by category:

- Templates
- Processes
- Tasks
- Snippets

#### Sharing Search Results

You can share search results with team members by clicking the clipboard icon at the end of the input field:

![Copying search results using the clipboard icon](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-copy-search-result-link.png)

### Top Bar Features

#### Language Settings

Tallyfy supports translation of both the platform interface and the content of your processes and tasks. Click the globe icon in the top bar to select your preferred language.

![Language selection dropdown menu](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-change-language.png)

#### Notifications

The bell icon in the top bar displays notifications for task assignments, comments, and other updates related to your processes and tasks.
