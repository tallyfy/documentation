## How to create a new organization on Tallyfy

Creating a user account and creating an organization are separate steps in Tallyfy, follow these:

1. Go to our website [https://tallyfy.com](https://tallyfy.com).

2. Click on TRY IT NOW. This will take you to the signup page [https://tallyfy.com/start/](https://tallyfy.com/start). Fill out the required fields and click submit. We highly recommend signing up using your Microsoft or Google accounts to prevent needing to remember another password.

3. Your account will be created in just a few seconds! We provide sample templates and step-by-step guidance to help you navigate Tallyfy.

If you have any questions, need a demo, or any assistance setting up your processes in Tallyfy, you can book a call with us at [https://tallyfy.com/booking](https://tallyfy.com/booking). We're here to help!
