## Country Restrictions

To ensure the safety and smooth operation of our product, certain countries are currently blocked from using Tallyfy. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.

If you're curious about whether your country is included in the list, you can check the official country codes [here](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_3166-1_alpha-2#Officially_assigned_code_elements).


The countries currently under restriction are:

* 'CU', 'IR', 'SY', 'KP', 'LY', 'SD', 'SO', 'VE', 'IQ', 'RU', 'YE', 'BY', 'PS'

Don't worry, we're open to discussing any issues or exceptions. If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to [contact us](https://tallyfy.com/contact-us/). We'll be more than happy to assist you!
