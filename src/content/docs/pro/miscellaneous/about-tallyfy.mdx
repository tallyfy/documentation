## Company overview

### Track record

Tallyfy has operated continuously since 2015 as a profitable, independent company without venture capital backing.

### Ownership structure

As an independent company without venture capital:
- Customer needs drive product decisions
- No pressure to sell or exit for investor returns
- Focus on sustainable growth and product quality

### Platform expertise

Tallyfy's founders combine process improvement expertise with technical development. The platform holds official US trademarks.

### Security standards

Tallyfy maintains:
- [SOC 2 Type 2 compliance](/products/pro/compliance/) with independent auditing
- [BIMI compliance](/products/pro/compliance/bimi-compliance/) for email authentication
- [HSTS compliance](/products/pro/compliance/hsts-compliance/) for encrypted connections

### Pricing model

Tallyfy offers a [Fair Price Guarantee program](/products/pro/pricing/) with consistent pricing regardless of geographic location.

Learn more at our [company page](https://tallyfy.com/about-tallyfy/)<a href="https://tallyfy.com/about-tallyfy/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><sup>[1]</sup></a>.

### Independence vs. venture capital

Industry statistics on venture-backed companies:

- 75% of VC-backed companies never return cash to investors<a href="https://www.hbs.edu/news/Pages/item.aspx?num=214" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><sup>[2]</sup></a> (Harvard Business School)
- 35% of Series A startups fail before Series B<a href="https://www.luisazhou.com/blog/startup-failure-statistics" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><sup>[3]</sup></a>
- VC exits to private equity often lead to higher prices and declining service<a href="https://www.ineteconomics.org/perspectives/blog/private-equity-is-out-of-control-and-looting-america-this-prosecutor-says-we-can-fix-it" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><sup>[4]</sup></a>
- Series A closures increased 61% in Q1 2024<a href="https://carta.com/data/startup-shutdowns-q1-2024/" rel="nofollow" target="_blank"><sup>[5]</sup></a>
