## How to take screenshots and screencasts for support tickets

Screenshots and screencasts can effectively communicate issues to Tallyfy Support, helping the team understand and resolve problems more efficiently.

Here's how you can take screenshots and screencasts:

**Taking screenshots:**

- For Mac users, press Shift-Command (âŒ˜)-3 to capture your screen.
- For Windows users, use the key combination Ctrl + PrtScn to take a screenshot.

**Taking screencasts/videos:**

To create a screencast, you can use one of these free tools:

1. [https://www.useloom.com/](https://www.useloom.com/) (add to Chrome)
2. [https://www.screencastify.com](https://www.screencastify.com/) (add to Chrome)
3. [http://recordit.co/](http://recordit.co/)

These tools allow you to capture and share your screen when illustrating issues or providing feedback to the support team.
