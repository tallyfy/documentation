## How to share screenshots and screencasts with Tallyfy support

Have you ever had an issue or wanted to give feedback to Tallyfy Support, but struggled to explain it in words? Well, we've got a solution for you! Sharing a screenshot or a screencast (video) can make a huge difference and help our support team understand and resolve your issue faster.

Here's how you can take screenshots and screencasts:

**Taking screenshots:**

- For Mac users, simply press Shift-Command (âŒ˜)-3 to capture your screen.
- For Windows users, use the key combination Ctrl + PrtScn to take a screenshot.

**Taking screencasts/videos:**

To create a screencast, you can use one of these free tools:

1. [https://www.useloom.com/](https://www.useloom.com/) (add to Chrome)
2. [https://www.screencastify.com](https://www.screencastify.com) (add to Chrome)
3. [http://recordit.co/](http://recordit.co/)

With these simple tools, you'll be able to capture and share your screen with ease. So the next time you need to illustrate your issue or feedback, give screenshots or screencasts a try - it'll save everyone time and make problem-solving a breeze!
