## How to share screenshots and screencasts with Tallyfy support

Screenshots and screencasts can effectively communicate issues to Tallyfy Support, helping the team understand and resolve problems more efficiently.

Here's how you can take screenshots and screencasts:

**Taking screencasts/videos:**

1. [https://www.useloom.com/](https://www.useloom.com/) (add to Chrome)
