## Contact the Tallyfy support team

Once you've signed up for Tallyfy, the support team is available to assist with questions and provide technical help.

Click on the **Get Help** bubble in the bottom right corner of your screen. You can send an email and include any relevant screenshots to help better understand your issue.

If you're looking for a more personalized experience, we also offer custom help through calls with our dedicated workflow and product experts. Use our support widget on the bottom right corner to create a ticket, or email support at-sign tallyfy dot-com.

You can also schedule a call directly via our [booking page](https://tallyfy.com/booking/).

:::tip[Free Live Support Calls]
Tallyfy believes in providing excellent support without hidden costs. As part of our "Workflow skills without the bills" approach, customers on paid plans can schedule **free, unlimited 25-minute screen-sharing calls** with our workflow experts using the booking link above. Get personalized help with integrations, template building, troubleshooting, or any other questions you have, without worrying about professional services fees for standard support needs.
:::

### Professional services

While Tallyfy support assists with many issues at no cost, certain situations involving significant effort may require professional services engagement. Tallyfy will inform you if professional services are recommended for your request. These paid services might include work in the following areas:

* **Dedicated account manager**. An expert who will know your needs inside and out.
* **Mapping your process(es)**. We make sure you have all the right steps and everyone agrees.\n
* **Optimizing your process(es)**. We make sure the [process](/products/pro/tracking-and-tasks/processes/) is as efficient and scalable as possible.\n
* **Create Tallyfy [templates](/products/pro/documenting/templates/)**. We use AI to generate a template from scratch or upload your existing document.\n
* **Testing and piloting**. We can help you define a pilot, a business case, ROI metrics and more.\n
* **Systems and [integration](/products/pro/integrations/)**. We evaluate your current systems and propose integration methods.\n
* **Change management and training**. We facilitate [launch](/products/pro/launching/), adoption and change management for maximum ROI.\n
