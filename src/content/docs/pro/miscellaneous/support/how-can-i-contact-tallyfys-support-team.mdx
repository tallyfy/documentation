## Contacting Tallyfy Support

Need help with Tallyfy? Our support team is here to assist you.

### Standard Support (Free)

There are several ways to get help:

*   **In-App Help Bubble**: Click the **Get Help** bubble (usually green, in the bottom right corner of Tallyfy). You can search help articles or send a message (including screenshots) to our support team.
*   **Email**: Send an email directly to `support@tallyfy.com`.
*   **Book a Call**: Schedule a free 25-minute screen-sharing call with a Tallyfy expert using our [booking page](https://tallyfy.com/booking/).

:::tip[Free Live Support Calls for Paid Plans]
We believe in providing great support. Customers on paid plans get **free, unlimited 25-minute support calls** via screen-share. Use the booking link to get personalized help with building templates, setting up integrations, troubleshooting, or any other questions â€“ at no extra charge.
:::

### Professional Services (Paid Engagement)

For more in-depth help that requires significant time or consulting, we offer paid Professional Services. Our support team will let you know if your request falls into this category. Examples include:

*   **Dedicated Account Manager**: Get an expert assigned specifically to your account.
*   **Process Mapping**: We help you map out complex processes before building them in Tallyfy.
*   **Process Optimization**: We help analyze and improve your workflow efficiency.
*   **Template Creation**: We can build complex templates for you.
*   **Pilot Programs & ROI**: Help defining test runs, measuring success, and calculating return on investment.
*   **Complex Integrations**: Assistance designing and testing connections to other systems.
*   **Change Management & Training**: Help rolling out Tallyfy to your team and ensuring successful adoption.
