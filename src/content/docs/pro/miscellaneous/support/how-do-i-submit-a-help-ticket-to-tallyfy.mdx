## How to submit a help ticket

If you're not finding what you need, don't worry! We're here to help. Just submit a help ticket to Tallyfy Support with your question, issue, or feature request. Our friendly team would be happy to assist you.

You can either email on support (at-sign) tallyfy dot com or you can click on the green bubble icon on the bottom right of all pages to create a ticket.
