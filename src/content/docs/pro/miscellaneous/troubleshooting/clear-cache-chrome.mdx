## How to clear your cache in Chrome

Clearing the cache in Chrome can help resolve display issues, loading problems, and outdated content in Tallyfy. Follow these steps:

1. Open Chrome on your computer.
2. Click the three dots icon (â‹®) in the top-right corner of the browser window.
3. Select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
4. Scroll down and click **Privacy and security** in the left sidebar.
5. Click **Clear browsing data**.
6. Select a time range from the dropdown menu. Choose **All time** for the most thorough clearing.
7. Check the following boxes:
   - **Browsing history**
   - **Cookies and other site data**
   - **Cached images and files**
8. Click **Clear data**.

After clearing your cache, close and reopen Chrome before returning to Tallyfy.

:::note[Common issues resolved by clearing cache]
Clearing your browser cache can fix:
- Pages not loading correctly
- Login problems
- Forms not submitting properly
- Outdated content appearing
:::

If clearing your cache doesn't resolve your Tallyfy issues, [contact Tallyfy support](https://tallyfy.com/contact-us/) for further assistance.
