## How to clear your cache in Safari(Desktop)

Clearing the cache in Safari can help resolve some of the most common issues with web applications such as Tallyfy. Here's how you can do it:

1. Open Safari.
2. Click on **Safari** in the top left corner of the screen.
3. Select **Preferences**.
4. In the dialog box that appears, click on the **Privacy** tab.
5. Click on **Manage Website Data**.
6. In the dialog box that appears, click on **Remove All**.
7. Click on **Remove Now**.

:::note[Getting more information]

If you want to learn more about clearing the cache in Safari, you can visit the [official Apple support page](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/clear-your-browsing-history-sfri47acf5d6/mac).

:::

That's it! Your cache is now cleared, and you should be able to use Tallyfy without any issues. If you're still experiencing problems, please feel free to [contact us](https://tallyfy.com/contact-us/). We'll be more than happy to assist you!
