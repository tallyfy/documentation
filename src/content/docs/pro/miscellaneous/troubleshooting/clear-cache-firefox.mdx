## How to clear your cache in Firefox

Clearing the cache in Firefox resolves performance issues, loading errors, and outdated content in Tallyfy. Follow these steps:

<Steps>
1. Open Firefox on your computer.
2. Click the menu button (â˜°) in the top-right corner of the browser window.
3. Select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
4. Select the **Privacy & Security** panel from the left sidebar.
5. Scroll down to the **Cookies and Site Data** section.
6. Click the **Clear Data...** button.
7. Ensure the **Cookies and Site Data** checkbox is selected.
8. Ensure the **Cached Web Content** checkbox is selected.
9. Click the **Clear** button.
10. When the confirmation dialog appears, click **Clear Now**.
</Steps>

After clearing your cache, restart Firefox before returning to Tallyfy.

:::note[Alternative method - keyboard shortcut]
You can also open the Clear History dialog by pressing **Ctrl+Shift+Delete** (Windows/Linux) or **Command+Shift+Delete** (Mac).
:::

If you continue experiencing issues with Tallyfy after clearing your cache, [contact Tallyfy support](https://tallyfy.com/contact-us/) for additional assistance.
