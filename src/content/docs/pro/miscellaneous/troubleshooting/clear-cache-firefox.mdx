## How to clear your cache in Firefox

Clearing the cache in Firefox can fix performance issues, loading errors, or outdated content in Tallyfy. Follow these steps:

<Steps>
1. Open Firefox on your computer.
2. Click the menu button (â˜°) in the top right corner.
3. Select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
4. Select the **Privacy & Security** panel on the left.
5. Scroll down to the **Cookies and Site Data** section.
6. Click the **Clear Data...** button.
7. Check the **Cookies and Site Data** box.
8. Check the **Cached Web Content** box.
9. Click the **Clear** button.
10. In the confirmation pop-up, click **Clear Now**.
</Steps>

After clearing the cache, close and reopen Firefox before trying Tallyfy again.

:::note[Alternative method: keyboard shortcut]
You can also open the 'Clear Recent History' dialog using `Ctrl+Shift+Delete` (Windows/Linux) or `Command+Shift+Delete` (Mac). Ensure 'Cache' is checked before clearing.
:::

If you still have issues with Tallyfy after clearing your cache, please [contact Tallyfy support](https://tallyfy.com/contact-us/) for more help.
