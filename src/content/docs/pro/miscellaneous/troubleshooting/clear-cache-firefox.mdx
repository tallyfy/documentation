## How to clear your cache in Firefox

Clearing the cache in Firefox can help resolve some of the most common issues with web applications such as Tallyfy. Here's how you can do it:

1. Open Firefox.
2. Click on the three horizontal lines in the top right corner of the browser window.
3. Select **Settings**.
4. In the left-hand menu, click on **Privacy & Security**.
5. Scroll down to the **Cookies and Site Data** section.
6. Click on **Clear Data**.
7. In the dialog box that appears, make sure that both **Cookies and Site Data** and **Cached Web Content** are checked.
8. Click on **Clear**.

:::note[Getting more information]

If you want to learn more about clearing the cache in Firefox, you can visit the [official Mozilla support page](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/how-clear-firefox-cache).

:::

That's it! Your cache is now cleared, and you should be able to use Tallyfy without any issues. If you're still experiencing problems, please feel free to [contact us](https://tallyfy.com/contact-us/). We'll be more than happy to assist you!
