## How to clear the Edge browser cache

Clearing your Microsoft Edge cache resolves display problems, loading issues, and outdated content in Tallyfy. Follow these steps:

<Steps>
1. Open Microsoft Edge on your computer.
2. Click the menu button (â‹¯) in the top-right corner of the browser window.
3. Select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
4. Click **Privacy, search, and services** in the left sidebar.
5. Under the **Clear browsing data** section, click **Choose what to clear**.
6. Select a time range from the dropdown menu. Choose **All time** for the most thorough clearing.
7. Check the following boxes:
   - **Browsing history**
   - **Cookies and other site data**
   - **Cached images and files**
8. Click **Clear now**.
</Steps>

After clearing your cache, restart Edge before returning to Tallyfy.

:::note[Alternative method - keyboard shortcut]
You can open the Clear browsing data panel by pressing **Ctrl+Shift+Delete** (Windows) or **Command+Shift+Delete** (Mac).
:::

### Clear cache for a specific site

If you want to clear the cache just for Tallyfy:

<Steps>
1. Visit the Tallyfy website.
2. Click the padlock icon in the address bar.
3. Click **Cookies**.
4. Select the Tallyfy cookies.
5. Click **Remove**.
6. Refresh the page.
</Steps>

If clearing your cache doesn't resolve your Tallyfy issues, [contact Tallyfy support](https://tallyfy.com/contact-us/) for additional assistance.
