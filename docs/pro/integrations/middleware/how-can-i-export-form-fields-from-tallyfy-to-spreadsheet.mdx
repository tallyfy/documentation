## How to stream form fields and other data from Tallyfy to a spreadsheet

You may want to collect data throughout the course of a process and send some of that information to another service or just store it on a cloud spreadsheet.

The video below walks you through how to export form field values from Tallyfy to a spreadsheet.

It uses Zapier as an example of middleware to integrate but you can achieve the same result  with Microsoft Power Automate or a variety of other middleware services and serverless functions.
