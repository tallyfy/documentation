## How to share screenshots and screencasts with Tallyfy support

Have you ever had an issue or wanted to give feedback to Tallyfy Support, but struggled to explain it in words? Well, we've got a solution for you! Sharing a screenshot or a screencast (video) can make a huge difference and help our support team understand and resolve your issue faster.
