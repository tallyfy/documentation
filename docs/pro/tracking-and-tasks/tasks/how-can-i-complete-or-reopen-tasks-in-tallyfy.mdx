## How to complete and reopen tasks

To complete a task, you have two simple options:

Option 1:

- Click on the checkmark to the left of your task.

Option 2:

- Open your desired task and select **Complete**.

![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-select-complete-task.png)

Now, your task will be marked as _Completed_. Awesome job! But what if you need to reopen a task? No worries! Simply select **Re-Open** to reverse it.

![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-reopen-task.png)
