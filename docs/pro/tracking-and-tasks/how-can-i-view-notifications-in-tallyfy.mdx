## Where are your in-app notifications

Notifications in Tallyfy are a way to keep you informed about important actions and updates. They appear in real-time at the top right corner of your screen.
![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-show-notifications.png)

To view all notifications, simply click on "VIEW ALL NOTIFICATIONS".
![Image](https://screenshots.tallyfy.com/tallyfy%2Fpro%2Fdesktop-light-show-all-notifications.png)
